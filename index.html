Frequently asked questions (FAQ)
How does it work?
Simply browse our cake menu, add items to your cart, enter your delivery address and preferred time slot, then place your order via WhatsApp. Pay via UPI after placing your order and send us the payment screenshot to confirm!
My pincode is not available, what can I do?
Currently we deliver within select areas. If your pincode is not covered, please reach out to us on WhatsApp — we'll try our best to accommodate you or suggest the nearest pickup option!
Is midnight delivery available?
Yes! Midnight delivery is available on special request. Please contact us on WhatsApp at least 24 hours in advance to arrange a midnight delivery for birthdays and surprise celebrations. Additional charges may apply.
How can I track my order?
Once your order is confirmed, our baker will keep you updated via WhatsApp. You'll receive updates when your cake is being prepared and when it's out for delivery. Feel free to message us anytime for a status update!
What is scheduled delivery?
Scheduled delivery lets you choose a specific time slot for your cake to arrive — Morning (9 AM – 1 PM) or Evening (4 PM – 8 PM). You can also request a specific date for advance orders placed at least 24 hours in advance.
Is there Cash on Delivery available?
Yes, Cash on Delivery (COD) is available for select areas. However, we encourage UPI payments (pookiecakes@upi) as they help us confirm your order faster. COD orders require confirmation via WhatsApp before dispatch.
How can I cancel the order?
You can cancel your order by messaging us on WhatsApp within 1 hour of placing it. Once baking has started, cancellations may not be possible. For advance orders, cancellations are accepted up to 12 hours before the delivery slot.
How can I update the address of my ongoing order?
Please contact us immediately on WhatsApp with your order details and the new delivery address. Address changes are accepted up to 2 hours before the scheduled delivery time, subject to the new address being within our delivery zone.
How much time is needed to deliver an order?
Standard orders placed before 12 PM can be delivered the same evening. For custom cakes or large orders, we recommend placing your order at least 24–48 hours in advance to ensure perfect preparation and timely delivery.
Is there any discount for new customers?
Yes! First-time customers get a special welcome discount. Message us on WhatsApp mentioning it's your first order and we'll share an exclusive offer just for you. 🎉 Follow us on social media for seasonal deals and festive discounts too!
Can I customise my cake?
Absolutely! You can add a custom message on your cake directly from our product page. For special designs, themes, or photo cakes, please contact us on WhatsApp with your requirements at least 48 hours in advance.
Can I hide the sender name of my order?
Yes! If you'd like to send a cake as a surprise gift anonymously, just let us know on WhatsApp when placing your order. We'll ensure the sender's name and any pricing details are removed from the packaging. 🎁
How can I be sure that fresh cakes are delivered?
All our cakes are freshly baked on the day of delivery by our home bakers. We use premium, fresh ingredients without preservatives. Our packaging ensures the cake stays intact and fresh during transit. Your satisfaction is our priority! 🌸
Can I order for my friend in another city?
Currently we operate within our local delivery zone. However, we're expanding! Contact us on WhatsApp with the delivery city — we'll let you know if we can arrange delivery or connect you with a partner baker in that area.
Can I order in Advance?
Yes! We highly recommend advance ordering for special occasions like birthdays and anniversaries. You can place orders up to 7 days in advance. Just select your preferred delivery date when you message us on WhatsApp after placing your cart order.

Still have questions? We're here to help! 🌸
